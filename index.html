Biography

I began my career as a theoretical physicist, specializing in computational neuroscience, while working on projects of various scale in many different domains such as finance, healthcare, and regulatory compliance. During this time, I actively participated in the academic community, contributing to research publications and releasing open-source tools.

After several years of research experience, I decided to pivot my career and take an active part in the development of a field I am very passionate about – artificial intelligence governance and regulatory compliance. To fulfill this goal, I transitioned from academia to industry, starting with a data science role. I chose to pursue leadership opportunities that would complement my technical skills with strategic business understanding.

In my current role, I successfully lead AI audit frameworks, developing comprehensive systems for regulated industries using advanced machine learning techniques and governance methodologies. I continued building expertise in regulatory compliance, filling in the gaps in practical implementation that I missed in academic settings.

Currently, I work as a data science manager leading teams that develop AI systems for regulated environments; contributing to compliance frameworks and experimenting with novel approaches to ethical AI deployment.

In 2024, I established myself as a leader in AI governance and regulatory ML systems.
